VibeGen Support

Welcome to the VibeGen Help Center. Find answers to common questions and get the support you need.

Getting Started

How does VibeGen work?
Select your location on the map, choose categories and styles, then generate personalized AI recommendations for your perfect spots.
Do I need an account?
You can use VibeGen without an account, but creating one lets you save favorites and manage your subscription.

Subscriptions

What plans are available?
Free: 3 AI recommendations per month. Pro: $2.99/month or $29.99/year for unlimited recommendations, unlimited location searches, and unlimited saved favorites.
How do I cancel my subscription?
Cancel anytime through App Store settings. Go to Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → VibeGen → Cancel. Your subscription remains active until the end of the billing period.
Can I get a refund?
Refunds are handled according to Apple's refund policy. You can request a refund through Apple's support system or your App Store account settings.
How do I restore my subscription?
If you've previously purchased a subscription, it should restore automatically when you sign in. If it doesn't, try signing out and back in, or contact support.

Features

How do I save recommendations?
Saving recommendations is a Pro feature. Tap the save icon on any recommendation to add it to your Collection. Pro subscribers get unlimited saves. Free users can use the app but cannot save items.
Can I add custom categories or styles?
Yes! Type your custom category or style in the input field and tap add to personalize your recommendations. You can add up to 5 custom categories and 10 custom styles.
How does the search history exclusion work?
If enabled, VibeGen automatically excludes places shown in your recent searches (last 7 days) to provide fresh recommendations. You can toggle this feature in the Discover screen. Your search history is automatically cleared every 3 months.
Why aren't my recommendations showing up?
Make sure you have available recommendations in your plan. Free users get 3 per month, and Pro users get unlimited. Check your subscription status in Settings.

Account

How do I reset my password?
On the sign-in screen, tap 'Forgot Password' and enter your email. You'll receive reset instructions via email.
How do I delete my account?
Go to Settings → Delete Account. This permanently removes all your data and cannot be undone.
Can I use VibeGen on multiple devices?
Yes! Sign in with the same account on multiple devices to sync your saved favorites and preferences.

Troubleshooting

The app is not loading recommendations
Check your internet connection, ensure location services are enabled, and verify you have available recommendations in your plan. Try restarting the app if issues persist.
Location services are not working
Make sure location permissions are enabled in your device settings. Go to Settings → Privacy → Location Services → VibeGen and ensure it's set to "While Using the App" or "Always".
The map is not displaying correctly
Ensure you have a stable internet connection and that Google Maps services are available in your region. Try closing and reopening the app.
I'm experiencing crashes or bugs
Try updating to the latest version of the app, restart your device, and ensure you have sufficient storage space. If problems persist, contact support with details about the issue.
